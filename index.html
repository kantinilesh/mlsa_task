Announcements

At DPS, we believe in keeping our community informed and engaged. Our regular announcements provide essential updates on school events, programs, and important dates. Here’s what’s coming up: Summer Camp Registration: DPSB is excited to offer a dynamic summer camp program, filled with activities that promote both learning and fun. Registration is open, and we encourage parents to sign up their children before the May 15 deadline. Parent-Teacher Conference: Our bi-annual Parent-Teacher Conference will be held on June 1. This event offers a valuable opportunity for parents to meet with teachers, discuss their childs progress, and collaborate on their educational journey. Annual Sports Day: On July 10, DPSB will host its much-anticipated Annual Sports Day. Students will participate in various sports events, fostering team spirit, discipline, and a healthy competitive attitude. All parents and guardians are invited to join us for a day of fun and celebration. These announcements are a glimpse of the vibrant life at DPSB, where we continuously strive to create a supportive and enriching environment for our students. Stay tuned for more updates!
